How the repayment is calculated

The deposit comes off first. The multiplier applies only to the balance.

First calculate the initial deposit from the confirmed phone price. Subtract that deposit to get the balance. The balance is then multiplied by the factor for your chosen duration.

1
Initial deposit

Phone price × required deposit percentage

2
Balance

Phone price − initial deposit

3
Total balance repayment

Balance × duration multiplier

4
Each instalment

Total balance repayment ÷ number of payments

1 month Balance × 1.2

This adds 20% of the balance as the plan cost. For monthly repayment, the full result is due in one payment.

2 months Balance × 1.4

This adds 40% of the balance as the plan cost. Monthly amount = total balance repayment ÷ 2.

3 months Balance × 1.6

This adds 60% of the balance as the plan cost. Monthly amount = total balance repayment ÷ 3.

Easy Buy FAQs

Questions before you apply?

What does Easy Buy mean?

Easy Buy lets eligible customers make a down payment on an iPhone and repay the balance in smaller installments over an approved period.

What is the minimum down payment?

It is 40% for iPhone 11 and 12 series, 50% for iPhone 13 through 16 series, and 70% for iPhone 17 series.

How often can I pay?

Monthly repayment is the standard option for every eligible iPhone. Weekly repayment is available only for iPhone 11 and 12 series. Your final schedule is confirmed during approval.

How is the repayment calculated?

The initial deposit is deducted from the confirmed phone price first. The remaining balance is multiplied by 1.2 for one month, 1.4 for two months, or 1.6 for three months. That total is then divided by the number of weekly, 14-day, or monthly payments.

How long can the plan run?

Easy Buy plans run for 1, 2, or 3 months, subject to verification and Formex’s final approval.

Who can be my reference?

Use a responsible person who knows you and can be reached by phone. Formex will explain any specific reference requirements during your application.

Does the calculator guarantee approval?

No. It is only a planning estimate. Formex will confirm the device price, any fees, repayment amount, dates, eligibility, and collection terms before you agree.
